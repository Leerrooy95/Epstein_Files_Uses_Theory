## 3. Blind Spots and Missed Connections

These are findings from my independent research that strengthen or extend the pattern documented in the three files:

### 3A. UNSC Resolution 2803 — The Board of Peace Is Now UN-Authorized

**What was missed**: The Board of Peace is not simply a Trump initiative operating outside international law. UN Security Council Resolution 2803 was passed (13 in favor, Russia and China abstaining) formally endorsing the Comprehensive Plan and authorizing the Board of Peace and an International Stabilization Force through December 31, 2027.

**Why it matters**: This transforms the BoP from a "pay-to-play club" (as The Guardian described it) into a body with UN Security Council backing. It also means Russia and China chose to *abstain* rather than veto — a significant diplomatic signal suggesting tacit acceptance of the framework.

**Connection to existing repo**: This connects to the `Repository_Synthesis.md` three-layer model. China's abstention on a resolution that could reshape Middle East governance aligns with the "alternative architecture" thesis — China may see the BoP as useful for its own positioning, even if it doesn't join.

**Sources**: [Wikipedia (Board of Peace)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Board_of_Peace), [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/02/07/gaza-board-peace-meeting-trump-dc)

---

### 3B. The Leaked BoP Resolution — "Emergency Powers" Over Gaza

**What was missed**: [Drop Site News](https://www.dropsitenews.com/p/leaked-board-of-peace-resolution-gaza-trump-us) obtained the unsigned "Resolution No. 2026/1" (dated January 22, 2026), which reveals:

- The Executive Board would assume **full legislative, executive, and judicial control** over Gaza
- Includes **"emergency powers"** provisions
- Palestinians relegated to "technocratic" implementation roles through the NCAG with **no decision-making power**
- The High Representative (Mladenov) supervises all Palestinian governance activity

**Why it matters**: The files document the *structure* of the BoP (tiers, membership fees, chairman powers) but miss the operational resolution that shows what the BoP would *do* with that authority. The leaked resolution makes concrete what the charter left abstract.

**Connection to the pattern**: This directly extends the "Financialization of Territory" observation. The governance architecture isn't merely pay-to-play membership — it's a framework for direct administrative control over Gaza by actors with documented financial interests in Israeli settlements.

**Source**: [Drop Site News](https://www.dropsitenews.com/p/leaked-board-of-peace-resolution-gaza-trump-us), [MR Online](https://mronline.org/2026/01/28/leaked-document-exposes-u-s-blueprint-for-total-control-over-gaza-dsn/)
